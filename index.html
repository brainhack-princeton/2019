Registration for the Brainhack Princeton 2019 is now open!

Brainhack Princeton 2019 is an official satellite event of the Brainhack Global 2019, organized by Cameron Craddock. The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: November 13-15, 2019

Costs: Free for members of the Princeton community

Location: Princeton, NJ, USA

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas as "issues" on the GitHub repository, and to submit a report describing their project to the 2019 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Join the Brainhack Mattermost for updated information on the developing hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.

Hosted at: Princeton Neuroscience Institute and Department of Psychology
