Registration for the Dartmouth Brainhack 2017 is now open!

Dartmouth Brainhack 2017 is part of Brainhack Global 2017, organized by Cameron Craddock. The goal of this event is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.



Dates: March 02-05, 2017

Location: Hanover, NH, USA

Registration: please register on Eventbrite

Thanks to our sponsors, the registration is free
and includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner.



Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2017 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
